About Us

How to Survive CS@WPI is a set of resources for WPI students to enhance the courseload and work being done as a Computer Science Student.

This information was crowd sourced from Computer Science Students from an array of the higher level CS Courses.

Explore and see what there is to offer!

If we are missing something, feel free to suggest your own resource below.
