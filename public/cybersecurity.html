Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the study within Computer Science on how to keep personal data and other sensetive infomation safe. It is a specialization that is increasingly important in the changing world.

WPI offers a number of courses focused on Cybersecurity. Some of the offerings include CS 4401 and CS 4801.
